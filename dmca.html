DMCA & Content Policy

Our Commitment to Intellectual Property Rights

Our Position

IPTV Nexus operates as a reseller and technical solutions provider. We do not host, store, or distribute any video content ourselves. Our service provides playlist credentials that connect to third-party servers and streams, which are not owned or managed by us.

We take intellectual property rights very seriously and are committed to complying with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). We have established the following procedure to handle infringement claims.

Procedure for Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you are a copyright holder or an agent thereof and believe that any content accessible via our service infringes your copyright, you may submit a notification pursuant to the DMCA by providing our Copyright Agent with the following information in writing:

  • A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or a person authorized to act on their behalf.
  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  • Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing, including specific channel names or content titles, and information reasonably sufficient to permit us to locate the material (e.g., channel number or category).
  • Your contact information, including your address, telephone number, and an email address.
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  • A statement that the information in the notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.

Designated Copyright Agent

Our Action

Upon receipt of a valid DMCA notification, we will take commercially reasonable steps to identify the allegedly infringing stream within the playlists we provide and will work with the upstream provider to have it removed or blocked. Please note that we cannot guarantee the removal of content from the third-party source servers, but we will ensure it is no longer accessible via the playlists we resell.
